★Description of Coaching
Coaching is partnership (defined as an alliance, not a legal business partnership) between the Coach and the Client in a thought-provoking and creative process that inspires the client to maximize personal and professional potential. It is designed to facilitate the creation/development of personal, professional or business goals and to develop and carry out a strategy/plan for achieving those goals.

★Code of Ethics
Coach agrees to maintain the ethics and standards of behavior established by the International Coach Federation (ICF) that can be found at HERE. It is recommended that you review the ICF Code of Ethics and the applicable standards of behavior.


★You can depend on your Coach to:

  • Be an encouraging guide dedicated to your success and helping you find the treasure within you.
  • Create and maintain a safe and trusting environment.
  • Challenge you to open your mind to new possibilities, delve into every option to grow your success.
  • Listen to you, challenge you to expand your thoughts and feelings to ensure we get the best out of you.
  • Maintain confidentiality with your words and information.


★As the mentee, you commit to:

  • Cultivate honesty within yourself, take an open and unguarded inventory of your current state before defining your future state.
  • Remain open to discovering new possibilities for yourself in your journey towards a more fulfilling life.
  • Commit to the objectives you and your Coach outline and agree to a reasonable and achievable path to your identified goals.
  • Take full ownership for your commitments and the timelines you and your Coach agree to.
  • Communicate honestly, openly, respectfully and directly.

★Confidentiality
Your personal information will be kept strictly confidential, including your session notes/recording unless information shared could cause harm to yourself or others or lead to legal proceedings as your Coach has a responsibility to report such things. You also understand that you have agreed to participate in your coaching training and therefore these sessions may be reviewed for training purposes by a representative of Pure Spiritual Intelligence / Pure Coach trainer (in which case, your name will be hidden).
